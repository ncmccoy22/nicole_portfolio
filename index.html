Profile

Nicole has always been a storyteller by nature, and thrives on good communication and collaborating with others.

Work Experience

For the last five years Nicole has worked as a self-employed and contract photographer. Her business ownership experience equipped Nicole with skills in content creation, communications, sales, copywriting, SEO analytics, marketing, and social media management.

Nicole has been a contract photographer for the Rivets & Roses Wedding Photography Studio since 2017, and is also a contributor to The Restaurant Project where she is a photographer and stylist for various local businesses.

Nicole has also been the art department team lead at Trader Joe's and managed weekly graphics projects, worked with staff and management to support the Trader Joe's brand, and created many in-store marketing materials.

In November of 2020 during COVID-19, I created floor decals for the St. Paul Trader Joe's store. The purpose of these decals was to guide customers safely through the front end of the store, maintaining proper in-store policies and procedures during this time.

I designed the circular "stand here" decals by first scanning and repurposing previous stickers which highlighted Trader Joe's products. I then used Adobe Photoshop to create the final decals, along with the arrows, which were designed to place in between the circular decals to further guide customers.

Once the floor decals were created, ordered, and shipped to the store, I placed each decal properly at the front end of the store, abiding by CDC guidelines at this time that suggest maintaining a distance of six feet between two people.
